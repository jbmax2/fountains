Fountain Benefits:

  • Soothing and Relaxing
  • Fountains create a soothing and relaxing environment. The sound of trickling or rushing water has a calming effect, providing a natural soundtrack for your outdoor space.
  • The white noise generated by flowing water can also help drown out other noises, such as traffic or city sounds.
    • Visual Interest:
  • A well-designed fountain instantly elevates your outdoor space. Whether it's a classic wall fountain or a modern glazed feature, the visual appeal draws attention and adds character.
    • Fountains become focal points, enhancing the overall aesthetics of your garden or patio.
    Wildlife Attraction:
  • Water features attract wildlife, such as birds, butterflies, and dragonflies. Birds are particularly drawn to water, and a fountain can provide a source of hydration and bathing. (Be mindful of chemicals you use if you have critters enjoying the new feature!)
  • Consider placing bird baths near your fountain to encourage your feathered friends.
    • Property Value:
  • A thoughtfully designed fountain can increase your property's value. Prospective buyers appreciate well-maintained outdoor spaces with attractive features.
  • If you're considering selling your home, a beautiful fountain can be a selling point.

    • Fountain Maintenence

    While fountains can be a beautiful addition to your yard they do require maintenence for a long lasting attraction.

    Regular Cleaning
  • Algae and other debris can build up in your fountain, affecting its appearance and water quality. Regular cleaning is essential to keep your fountain looking its best.
  • Drain and clean your fountain every few weeks, or as needed. Use a mild detergent such as Simple Green and a soft brush to scrub away dirt and grime.
    • Pump Care
  • The pump is crucial for your fountains operation. Regularly check for debris build up and aedequite water supply. Dry fountains are deadly for your pump!
  • Regularly check the hose lines to make sure there is no kinks or blockages.
    • Algae Control:
  • Algae thrives in hot summer months. See our Garden Center for wildlife-safe products to help keep your fountain sparkling clean!
    • Mineral Deposits:
  • Hard-water stains appear as crusty, white buildup on the fountain's surface.
  • Scrub off deposits using white vinegar during your cleaning?
